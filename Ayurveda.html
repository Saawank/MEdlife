Ayurveda

Fever

Ayurvedic solution

Preferred by Doctors

   

  • 1. Turmeric and dried ginger Powder
  • 2. Coriander Tea
  • 3. Lemon and honey

Headache

Ayurvedic solution

Preferred by Doctors

   

  • 1. Chandan or sandalwood
  • 2. Biting into choti elaichi is also an effective way
  • 3. Brahmi: The cooling herb is known to cut down stress

Cold and Cough

Ayurvedic solution

Preferred by Doctors

   

  • 1.Consume turmeric powder mixed with honey
  • 2.Drink tea made of Tulsi (Basil leaves), ginger and honey
  • 3.Add a pinch of cinnamon powder in warm water or tea

Stomachache

Ayurvedic solution

Preferred by Doctors

   

  • 1. Hing (Asafoetida) and ghee on stomach
  • 2. Decoction of jeera (cumin) and water
  • 3. Yoghurt is considered to be a very good pro-biotic.
